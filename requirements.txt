Software Requirements Specification (SRS)
 Online Sports & Facility Management System
1. Introduction
1.1 Purpose
This document specifies the requirements for the Online Sports & Facility Management System intended for
government or institutional use.
1.2 Scope
The system enables verified users to book sports facilities through a centralized web portal with strict
administrative control.
2. Overall Description
2.1 Product Perspective
A web-based centralized management system operated under administrative authority.
2.2 Product Functions
User registration, facility booking, payment handling, rule enforcement, and admin management.
3. Functional Requirements
 User Registration with Aadhaar and photo verification.
 Time slot booking with fixed schedules.
 Single game or membership payment.
 Admin approval and revocation authority.
4. Non-Functional Requirements
 Security of personal data.
 High availability during booking hours.
 Government-standard UI and compliance.
5. Constraints
Fixed time slots, mandatory identity verification, and admin override authority.
6. Future Enhancements
QR-based entry, notifications, analytics dashboard, and priority booking.
End of Document
